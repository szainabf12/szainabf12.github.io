About Us

Indago Clinical Research has been providing clinical research services to the global biopharmaceutical industry for many years in United States. We offer clinical pharmacology expertise to guide and support development plans when entering clinical trials.


As a full-service clinical research facility, we have extensive expertise in the execution of a wide range of Phase I - Phase IV studies, especially complex clinical pharmacology trials, single ascending dose studies, multiple ascending dose studies, bioavailability and bioequivalence studies in healthy normal volunteers and patient populations to randomized, blinded, placebo controlled, parallel cohort studies and open label studies as well.


Our study teams are led by highly experienced Principal Investigators intricately involved in all aspects of the clinical trial process.
